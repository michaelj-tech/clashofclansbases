Types of Clash of Clans Bases

Attackers follow different attacking strategies to loot resources, collect trophies, and earn stars so players require strong defense against all the unique attacks. Some players want to protect the town hall, others want to secure resources, some focus on preventing attackers from getting trophies, and others show strength and reflect their offence.

Our team provides you with a huge collection of war, farming, hybrid, trophy, anti-air, anti 2 star, anti 3 star, defense, funny, art, progress, anti-loot, anti-ground, anti everything, and all other types of COC Bases.

Farming Bases

Farming bases are designed particularly to protect resources. We design the bases for players who focus on farm by placing gold, elixir, and dark elixir storages in the central compartments.

War/CWL Bases

War bases are designed to protect the Town Hall, especially in wars and CWL/Clan War Leagues. These bases are further categorized into anti 2 star and anti 3 star bases. Our team designs extremely hard bases to prevent the attackers from easily earning 2-star or 3-star victory.

Trophy Bases

Trophy bases never let the enemies earn more trophies and move up in leagues. This can be done by preventing the attackers from getting 50% destruction. We provide a wide range of anti trophy bases for all town halls.

Hybrid Bases

Focusing on resource protection and also preventing the high star victory needs combined features farm, trophy, and war bases. This is possible with hybrid base links and players can have a strong defense with hybrid layout designs.

Anti Air Bases

Bases designed specifically to defend the village against common offensive strategy, air attacks are known as anti-air bases. These are also known as anti-dragon, anti-electro, or anti-balloon bases. We offer an extensive collection of anti air bases for all TH levels.

Funny/Art Bases

Fun loving players usually use art/funny bases to showcase creativity, troll the attacker, and sometimes participate in Clash of Clans community events.
